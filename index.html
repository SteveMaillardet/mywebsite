Bacchus

Bacchus was inspired by a TV programme on how civilisation has been influenced through the ages by wine. It is a collaboration by Steve Maillardet (Music) and Joel Rockman (Lyrics) and is in the style of Rock/Funk from the psychedelic era. A hypnotic song and musically a blast from the past!
